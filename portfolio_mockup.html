Frequently Asked Questions

What is your primary area of expertise?

I specialize in full-funnel performance marketing. This means I don't just run ads on Meta or Google; I build and optimize the entire user journey, from ad creative to landing page conversion rate optimization (CRO), ensuring maximum ROI.

Do you work with B2B or E-commerce?

Both. The fundamental principles of direct-response marketing apply universally. For B2B, the focus is typically on lead quality, CRM integration, and pipeline velocity. For E-commerce, it's about CPA, average order value (AOV), and LTV.

Can you handle creative design as well?

I write all direct-response copy and build wireframes. I am proficient in Figma and Canva for ad creatives, but for high-end video or complex graphics, I partner with or direct specialized creative teams.
